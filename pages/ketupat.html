Ketupat: Hari Raya Rice Cake

Ketupat or rice cakes are popular sides made of rice that often accompany Indonesian savory dishes, such as sate, gado-gado, ketoprak, lontong sayur, and many other dishes.

For festive occasions, we usually have ketupat wrapped in woven palm leave pouches in the shape of diamond

How To Make Ketupat

Ingredients

  • 500g rice
  • 30 young coconut leaves
  • Water

To Prep

  1. Weave the coconut leaves into a ketupat basket
  2. Open one of the edges and fill up the ketupat with raw rice until â…“ full, then reseal

To Cook

  1. Bring water to the boil in a large pot
  2. Gently lower the ketupat into the boiling water
  3. Allow to cook for 4 hrs
  4. Remove from the water, then allow to cool completely before slicing and serving
