Sambel Goreng Kentang: A Spicy Kick

It is one of the popular Javanese dishes that often served with opor ayam, sayur lodeh, rice or lontong/ketupat to make lontong cap go meh. \

But of course, you can always serve it with just plain rice too.

How To Make Sambel Goreng Kentang

Ingredients

  • 500 gr Russet potatoes peeled and diced
  • 500 gr chicken livers and/or gizzards boiled until cooked through and then cut into smaller pieces
  • 100 mll coconut cream (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • Oil for deep-frying the potato if you choose to deep-fry instead of roasting

Herbs:

  • 2 bay leaves (Indonesian: daun salam)
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves (Indonesian: daun jeruk)
  • 1 stalk lemongrass
  • 1 inch fresh galangal (Indonesian: lengkuas)

Seasonings:

  • 1 Tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Spice Paste:

  • 5 shallot (Indonesian: bawang merah)
  • 4 cloves garlic (Indonesian: bawang putih)
  • 1 inch ginger (Indonesian: jahe)
  • 5 Fresno peppers

To Prep

  1. Grind the all of the spice paste ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add livers and cook about 10-15 minutes. Remove from the pot and briefly rinse
  3. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add gizzards and cook about 30-45 minutes. Remove from the pot and briefly rinse
  4. If you choose to deep-fry the potato, heat up enough oil to do so and fry the potatoes until they are crispy

To Cook

  1. In a large skillet, preheat 2 Tbsp of cooking oil
  2. Add in the chili paste you grind earlier and saute for about 3 minutes
  3. Add the herbs and saute for another 3 minutes
  4. Add the cooked potatoes and livers and/or gizzard pieces and seasonings
  5. Stir to make sure the sambal is coating everything
  6. If you want to add coconut cream, add it here and let it cook until it's thickened
